Game Description
Embark on an extraordinary journey as an ambitious Shopkeeper in this captivating mobile RPG! Rise to the top by crafting and trading powerful items, traveling the world, and forging new alliances. Prepare to battle menacing monsters, discover rare materials, and attract eager customers.
What I Did
I established the project's source control setup and contributed to both system-level and gameplay code across the entire development cycle. I kickstarted the core gameplay systems, including the Battle System, Shop Simulation, and Crafting System. Working closely with the design team, I helped bring gameplay ideas to life, often leveraging advanced 3D math to achieve precise behaviors and mechanics. I led the user interface implementation, integrating animations, timelines, and Cinemachine for dynamic camera control. To ensure a smooth player experience, I also conducted thorough performance profiling and optimization.
