Royal Forest Battle
RPG Game for IOS
Engine:   Unity
Programming Languages:   C#, Ruby
Tools Used: Rider, Git, Svn
Link: Metacritic
Game Description
Royal Knight, enter the mysterious forests and face your opponents. You are not alone, the further you get the more support you can spawn against the oncoming waves of enemies. Spawn supporting units, get new perks, and level up your skills. How far can you get?
Features: - Face off against a variety of opponents and epic bosses - Spawn archers, knights, pyromancers, and frosters to support you in battle - Make your knight a true hero with unique upgrades - Play through hundreds of levels. Full text.
What I Did
Prototyped battle mechanics and unit traversal in Unity. Designed and implemented test levels to support gameplay experimentation. Researched Unity's ECS (Entity Component System) and conducted performance analysis to assess its viability.
