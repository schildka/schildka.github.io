Game Description
Merge Jelly Idle Adventure is a delightful puzzle adventure RPG that will captivate players with its charming jelly characters and engaging gameplay. Developed by The Phoenix Lighthouse GmbH, this free game is available on Android and offers a lighthearted storyline filled with mad robots and jelly heroes.
In Merge Jelly Idle Adventure, players join Ada, a mad scientist who accidentally created a group of pesky bots. It's up to the gang of jellys to save the world by merging and becoming stronger. The game features a variety of jellys in different shapes and colors, each with their own adorable design. Full review text.

Researched and integrated rewarded video ads into the game. Created a custom BigInt class to support endless progression mechanics.
