Game Description
In the VR game MILGRAM ASYLUM, players take on the role of a prisoner held in a sci-fi facility run by the organization SysRec. To earn the prospect of an early release, the prisoner must complete a series of experimental challenges.
The decisions players face throughout the game are based on ethical dilemmas, situations in which no option is entirely right or wrong. Each choice forces the player to confront moral ambiguity, with no clear or satisfying resolution. How a player responds is entirely unpredictable and shapes their unique experience.
Will they find a way to escape the prison or become a part of it?.
