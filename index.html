About Me
With a deep passion for gaming and a strong ambition to grow as a software developer, I’ve always been drawn to the challenge and excitement of creating games.
During my time at Kaiko (THQ Nordic), I played a key role in an AA-Project, coding according to the Entity-Component-System (ECS) pattern to ensure clean, maintainable, and scalable code. I collaborated closely with other departments to align technical implementation with creative vision and actively supported project management efforts. Applying advanced 3D mathematical concepts, I tackled intricate gameplay challenges while mentoring Junior Programmers and Interns in planning and writing structured code. Through regular performance analyses and optimization, I helped deliver a smooth and engaging player experience. This multifaceted experience allowed me to grow both professionally and creatively.
I completed my B.Sc. in Computer Science at the Frankfurt University of Applied Sciences in 2017, where I gained extensive experience in various programming languages. My passion for the game industry led me to pursue a degree in Games Engineering at the University of Würzburg in the same year. Throughout this program, I expanded my expertise in C++, C#, and various game engines through a range of projects.
